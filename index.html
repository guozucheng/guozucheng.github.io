A Pachycephalosaurus Movie

'Pachycephalosaurus: The Thick-Headed Lizard'. It's an excellent book, we think it is even more fun to make it into a movie. Sometimes, I animated the charactors and Dad held the camera, sometimes we switched our roles. Special thanks to my toy Antonov-225, who played dragonfly in the movie.
